What is APEX?

APEX stands for Athlete Performance Evaluation Index. The "X" represents the "X factor" ‚Äì proprietary data collected by evaluators that determines corrective measures and prescriptions.

APEX is a collaborative group of sports professionals, scouts, and recruiters including 8 college ADs working to combat the youth sports injury epidemic.

The Problem We're Solving

  • 33 million injuries per year in youth sports
  • Only 75 million kids play youth sports total
  • ACL injuries in girls at historic highs
  • Tommy John surgeries in kids as young as 10-12
  • 52% of kids leave sports before JV/varsity level

Core Philosophy

Foundation First, Performance Last:

  1. Movement Assessment (Foundation)
  2. Strength & Conditioning
  3. Intangibles (Nutrition, Mental)
  4. Performance (LAST)

Assessment Components

1. BASE Movement Assessment

Biomechanics, Athleticism, Sequence, Endurance

2. Strength & Conditioning

Soft tissue, flexibility, balance, foot speed, movement skills, core, total body

3. Nutrition Assessment

Online component requiring parent involvement

4. Mental/Neuro Assessment

Mental performance, health awareness, anxiety/depression screening
